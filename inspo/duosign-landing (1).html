The Pipeline

From words to motion
in three steps

DuoSign's modular architecture processes your text and drives a real-time 3D avatar — all in under a second.

01
Text to Gloss

A hybrid NLP engine — rule-based spaCy for 75–85% of phrases in under 50ms, with an LLM fallback for edge cases.

spaCy + LLM
02
Pose Lookup

Each token maps to a curated pose sequence from WLASL (21,000 videos). MediaPipe extracts 523 landmarks per frame.

MediaPipe · WLASL
03
Avatar Animation

Kalidokit converts pose data to VRM bone rotations. Three.js renders a smooth, accessible avatar in any browser.

Kalidokit · Three.js · VRM

What's Inside

Built for real-world use

Voice Input

Browser-native speech recognition lets you speak to translate. Zero extra setup — just talk and watch DuoSign sign it back.

Chrome Extension

Select any text on any website, right-click, and see a floating ASL panel. Brings sign language to the whole web.

REST API

POST English text, get back ASL gloss arrays and pose sequences. Simple JSON interface, fast response times, SDKs coming soon.

Translation History

Every translation is logged with timestamps, source type, and replay. Filter by voice or typed input at a glance.

2,000+ Signs

WLASL lexicon with 2,000+ ASL glosses, enriched with ASL-LEX phonological similarity data for intelligent fallback matching.

Guest Access

Jump in without an account. Guests get 10 free translations per session — sign up for unlimited access and saved history.
